Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

Pixy Dust Seasoning ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Order information — name, shipping address, email, and phone number provided when you place an order.
  • Payment information — processed securely by our payment provider. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Contact form submissions — any information you voluntarily provide when reaching out to us.
  • Usage data — pages visited, browser type, and general location (country/region) collected via analytics tools.
  • Cookies — small data files used to remember cart contents and site preferences. See our Cookie Policy for details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To send order confirmation and shipping updates
  • To respond to inquiries and support requests
  • To improve our site and understand how it is used
  • To send marketing emails if you have opted in (you may unsubscribe at any time)

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties who help us operate our business (e.g., payment processors, shipping carriers, email platforms), solely for those purposes and subject to confidentiality obligations.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@pixydustseasoning.com.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@pixydustseasoning.com.
