Cookie Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Pixy Dust Seasoning uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit — such as your cart contents — to make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and localStorage for the following purposes:

Type Purpose Duration
Essential / Functional Remembers your cart contents and site preferences (stored in localStorage as pixy_cart_v2) Until cleared
Analytics Helps us understand how visitors use the site (page views, traffic sources) Session / up to 2 years
Marketing Used by advertising platforms to deliver relevant ads (only if you consent) Up to 90 days

Third-Party Cookies

Some pages on our site may include content from third parties (such as embedded videos or social sharing buttons) that set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies. Please refer to the relevant third-party privacy policies for more information.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the site, including your shopping cart. Instructions for common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
