Shipping

Everything you need to know about how your order gets to you.

Domestic Shipping

  • Standard shipping: 5–7 business days
  • Expedited shipping: 2–3 business days
  • Overnight: 1 business day (where available)
  • Orders ship Monday–Friday, excluding holidays

Free Shipping

  • All Gift Sets ship free — no minimum required
  • Orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping
  • Free shipping applied automatically at checkout

Processing Time

Most orders are processed within 1–2 business days. During peak seasons or promotions, processing may take up to 3 business days. You will receive a tracking confirmation email once your order has shipped.

Order Tracking

A tracking number is emailed as soon as your order ships. If you did not receive a tracking email, check your spam folder or contact us at support@pixydustseasoning.com.

Questions about your order?

Our team is happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 1 business day.
