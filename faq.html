Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about Pixy Dust.

Products & Blends

What is the difference between pouches and bottles?

Pouches are our signature large-format blends at $13.00 each — perfect for frequent use or gifting. Bottles are a standard size starting at $7.95, ideal for trying a new blend or gifting alongside other items.

Are the blends gluten-free?

Most Pixy Dust blends are naturally gluten-free. Check the individual product page for full ingredient information. When in doubt, contact us at support@pixydustseasoning.com.

Do you offer a sugar-free option?

Yes. Our Sugar-Free All-Purpose blend is specifically formulated without added sugars — great for keto, low-carb, and diabetic-friendly cooking.

How long do the blends stay fresh?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Pouches and bottles are shelf-stable for 18–24 months. Best before date is printed on each package.

Can I use these blends as a dry rub and a finishing seasoning?

Absolutely. Most blends are engineered to work at both stages. See the Recipes page for guidance on how to use each blend at its best.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days. Expedited options (2–3 days) are available at checkout. See our Shipping page for full details.

Do gift sets ship free?

Yes — all gift sets (3-blend, 6-blend, and full 9-blend collection) ship free with no minimum order required.

Can I track my order?

Yes. A tracking number is emailed to you as soon as your order ships. If you didn't receive it, check your spam folder or contact us.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days on unopened items. Damaged items are eligible for a full refund or replacement. See our Returns page for the full policy.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing?

Yes. We work with restaurants, retailers, and hospitality programs. Visit our Wholesale page to submit an application.
